BankControlStructures Project 

1. create_tables.sql
   - Creates Customers and Loans tables with MySQL types.

2. insert_sample_data.sql
   - Inserts sample customer and loan data.

3. apply_discount_above_60.sql
   - Applies 1% discount to interest rates for customers over 60.

4. promote_vip_customers.sql
   - Sets IsVIP = TRUE for customers with balances over $10,000.

5. loan_due_reminders.sql
   - Shows loans due within the next 30 days.
